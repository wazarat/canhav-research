# Image Specifications for About Us Page Sections

## Required Images for About Us Page

### 1. Research Impact Image
**File Name:** `research-impact.jpg` or `research-impact.png`
**Location:** `/public/images/sections/`
**Usage:** Section 2 - "From Research to Real-World Impact"

**Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 600x480 pixels (5:4 aspect ratio)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 200KB (optimized for web)
- **Content Suggestions:**
  - Queen's University campus or innovation centre
  - Research/academic environment
  - Innovation/collaboration imagery
  - Professional academic setting
  - Technology/research lab setting

**Technical Requirements:**
- High resolution for crisp display
- Good contrast and lighting
- Professional appearance
- Fits purple/indigo color scheme

---

### 2. Recognition Image  
**File Name:** `recognition.jpg` or `recognition.png`
**Location:** `/public/images/sections/`
**Usage:** Section 3 - "Recognition Within the Global Crypto Ecosystem"

**Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 600x480 pixels (5:4 aspect ratio)  
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 200KB (optimized for web)
- **Content Suggestions:**
  - Award ceremony or recognition event
  - Coin Pitchfest event imagery
  - Professional achievement setting
  - Crypto/blockchain conference
  - Trophy, certificate, or award imagery

**Technical Requirements:**
- High resolution for crisp display
- Good contrast and lighting
- Professional appearance  
- Fits orange/red color scheme

---

## Current Display Containers

Both images will be displayed in:
- **Container Size:** 320x240px on desktop
- **Responsive:** Scales down on mobile
- **Styling:** Rounded corners, shadow effects
- **Background:** Gradient overlays (purple for research, orange for recognition)

## File Paths in Code

The images should be saved as:
1. `/public/images/sections/research-impact.jpg` (or .png)
2. `/public/images/sections/recognition.jpg` (or .png)

And will be referenced in the code as:
- `src="/images/sections/research-impact.jpg"`
- `src="/images/sections/recognition.jpg"`
